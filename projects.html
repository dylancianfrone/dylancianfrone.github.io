*Untitled Game*

Ryan Rose, Kevin Huynh, Dylan Cianfrone

In this game, the player controls a flock of bees which are controlled using
boid forces which are attracted to an invisible sphere that the player moves
using the arrow keys and mouse. Players collect pollen from flowers, and the
swarm grows until it hits the upper limit (Maximum Bees). After that, the
uses the remaining time to collect more pollen to get the highest score they
can. Built in Unity, Dec 2019.
